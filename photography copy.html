THROUGH MY LENS

I've been taking photos of my life since 2011. This includes people and places, but I specialize in food photography. You can find some of my work on websites like Eater Seattle, Zagat, or my blog Cake Potato.

Photography has always been a large part of my life-- it is a medium by which I could express myself and demonstrate my interpretation of the world around me.

Images are a powerful tool that transcend the barriers of language and distance. My goal is to create meaningful work to inspire and express.
