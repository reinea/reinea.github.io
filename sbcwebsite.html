'SISTERS BAKING COMPANY' WEBSITE

   
      

Sisters Baking Company is an Edmonds-based granola business run by two-- you guessed it-- sisters Jen and Jamie. They boast using only the best ingredients to produce a quality, crunchy, caramel-y granola bark that can now be purchased around the Greater Seattle Area. But like any growing business, they saw the need for an informative, modern website. They approached me looking for a fresh, minimalist theme that can showcase their product.

  
    
   
    

CONCEPT + DESIGN

   
      

The original website's primary flaws were that it was cluttered and difficult to navigate. Opting for a cleaner look, I designed a more photo-friendly design. Instead of telling their customers about how good the granola looks, the site can show them. By removing outdated pages and cutting down the unnecessary content, we were able to make the photos shine through. This new design is fuss-free and clean with a concept as simple as their granola's ingredients.
