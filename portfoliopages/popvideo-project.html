SPRING 2017

POP VIDEO TUTORIAL

Marvel POP, iMovie

I was fortunate enough to be part of a directed research group at the University of Washington the spring quarter of 2017. Our goal was to plan in-class design charettes for high-school and middle-school students across the Seattle area. We wanted to inform them about the growing department of Human-Centered Design and Engineering, so we provided a hands-on experience to help them understand our area of study a little better.


Part of our activity required them to prototype their designs using Marvel's POP (Prototyping on Paper) app. With our limited classroom time, I had to create a very brief how-to video that was easy to understand.
