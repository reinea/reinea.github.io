AUTUMN 2017

ROADIE APP

User-Centered Design Process and App Conceptualization

In my Introduction to User-Centered Design class in autumn quarter, we were assigned teams and given the freedom to choose a particular user group to design for. We were led through a 10-week user-focused process that resulted in a high-fidelity prototype.


Our concept focuses on serious hobbyist musicians who want better sound control over their performances because our research showed that inconsistencies in sound balance led to decreased confidence. The resulting project is a hypothetical solution that relies on future technologies such as digital mixing consoles.


This project was created in collaboration with my teammates Arsh Saroya, Pari Gabriel, and Sierramatice Karras.
